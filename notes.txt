command line to use for fluidsynth
fluidsynth -a pulseaudio -o synth.midi-bank-select=xg " - Soundfont Path - " " - Intro Midi - "

eg
fluidsynth -a pulseaudio -o synth.midi-bank-select=mma "/usr/share/sounds/sf2/GeneralUser GS 1.47/GeneralUser GS v1.47.sf2" "/usr/share/sounds/sf2/GeneralUser GS 1.47/demo MIDIs/To Make the End of Battle.mid"
